Chit Funds & How They Work

Chit funds are a type of savings and credit scheme where a group of people contribute a fixed amount periodically and receive a lump sum amount either through bidding or in a pre-decided order. Here's how it works:

  • Members contribute a fixed amount every month.
  • At every interval, a member gets a chance to receive the lump sum amount.
  • The remaining members continue to contribute until the end of the scheme.
